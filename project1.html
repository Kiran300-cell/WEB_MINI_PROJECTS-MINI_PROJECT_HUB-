All-Terrain Adapting Wheelchair

A next-generation adaptive wheelchair engineered for smooth mobility across all terrains.

The All-Terrain Adapting Wheelchair Model is a prototype designed to move smoothly across rough and uneven surfaces. Its multi-wheel adaptive mechanism maintains stability on slopes, gravel, grass, and outdoor terrain. Lightweight and modular, it enhances outdoor accessibility and serves as a base for future smart mobility upgrades.
